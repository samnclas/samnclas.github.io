TABLE TENNIS SCORING

In table tennis, a player or players wins a point when the opponent is unable to hit the ball with a racket over the net and onto the other side of the table.

On the other hand, a player wins a  game if he is the first player to win 11 points, and be at least 2 points ahead of his or her  opponent.

In the instance that both players in a table tennis match have scored 10 points, then the first player to get a 2 point lead wins the game.

A table tennis match is the best of 5 games.
