Answer:

3. how do you add a footer to a slide​


Answer:

Click INSERT > Header & Footer.

On the Slide tab, check Footer.

In the box below Footer, type the text that you want, such as the presentation title.

Check Date and time to add that to your slides.

Check Slide number to add that to your slides.

To stop the footer from appearing on the title slide, check Don't show on title slide.

Click Apply to All. Or, if you want the footer information only on the selected slide, click Apply instead of Apply to All.

4. When do you need collaborators for your google site


Answer:

Collaborators are useful for a Google Site when you want to work on the site with other people or when you want to give others permission to view, edit, or manage the site. Here are some situations where you might need collaborators for your Google Site:

You're working on a project with a team, and you want everyone to have access to the site to contribute and make changes.

You're creating a site for a group or organization, and you want to give members or administrators access to the site to manage content or update information.

You're using a site for a class or workshop, and you want to give students or participants access to the site to view resources, complete assignments, or collaborate on projects.

You're creating a site for your business or organization, and you want to give employees or contractors access to the site to help with content creation or site maintenance.

In general, if you want others to contribute to or manage your Google Site, you will need to add them as collaborators.
