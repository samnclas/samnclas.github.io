In your activity notebook, list down the other elements of arts and explain how they help in conveying the message of an artwork. Add rows for your answer. . Elements Explanation​


Answer:

1. Line

To put it simply, a line is an identifiable path created by a point moving in space

2. Shape

In art, line and shape almost always go together, and they have a lot of the same qualities. Shapes play an important role in the creation of art.

3. Color

Colors have three main characteristics— hue (blue, red, green, etc.), value (spectrum of light and dark) and intensity (spectrum of bright and dark) — all contributing to what the color communicates and how it is used.

4. Typography

Typography incorporates other elements of art, such as sizing, color, and spacing, to communicate an intended message.

5. Texture

Texture is everywhere and there are endless ways to describe it. You learn from a young age what it means for something to be rough, smooth, fuzzy, etc.

6. Size

Size, which is commonly associated with scale, is an important tool for artists to add emphasis and interest to their designs.

7. Space

In art, space refers to how a piece of artwork is organized – the area above, below, and within components of a piece.
