25. explain how the sky appears color blue in our eyes during daytime and red orange during sunset and sunrise​


Science

Answer

A clear cloudless day-time sky is blue because molecules in the air scatter blue light from the sun more than they scatter red light. When we look towards the sun at sunset, we see red and orange colors because the blue light has been scattered out and away from the line of sight. The sky's colors are affected by the molecules that scatter in the air, which is why the sky appears blue in the daytime and red orange during sunset and sunrise.
