29. what is peter oliva significant contribution to curriculum development?


Peter Oliva's significant contribution to curriculum development is constructing a curriculum model named after himself, the Oliva Model of Curriculum. This model serves as a framework from which curriculum models will be based depending on the learners, values, and their needs.

It focuses on the fact that educators should actively participate in making changes to their curriculum that is suitable for their district and classroom level.
