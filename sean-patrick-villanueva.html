16. The difference between shayne's and Sean's age 4. Thrice shayne's age increased by Sean's age the result is 44. Find their present age​


==========================

[tex]{ \large{ \bold{ {GIVEN:}}}}[/tex]

: The difference between Shayne's and Sean's age is 4. Thrice Shayne's age increased by Sean's age the result is 44. Find their present age.

[tex]{ \large{ \bold{ {ANSWER:}}}}[/tex]

☞ Shayne is currently 10 years old, while Sean is currently 14 years old.

Note: I used TRIAL AND ERROR METHOD.So let's say, Shayne is 10 years old while Sean is 14 years old.

[tex]{\boxed{\texttt{14 - 10 = 4}}}[/tex]

As we can see, the difference is 4. Now, let us multiply Shayne's age.

[tex]{\boxed{\texttt{10 × 3 = 30}}}[/tex]

Then, let us add the thrice of Shayne's age and Sean's age.

[tex]{\boxed{\texttt{30 + 14 = 44}}}[/tex]

==========================

21. who is Saint Patrick?​


Answer:

Saint Patrick is the patron saint of Ireland and is celebrated on March 17 every year as Saint Patrick's Day. He is credited with bringing Christianity to Ireland in the 5th century AD. According to legend, he used the three-leafed shamrock to explain the concept of the Holy Trinity (Father, Son, and Holy Spirit) to the Irish people. He is also known for performing miracles and driving the snakes out of Ireland, although this is likely a metaphor for driving out pagan beliefs. Saint Patrick's Day is celebrated in Ireland and around the world with parades, green clothing, and traditional Irish food and drink
