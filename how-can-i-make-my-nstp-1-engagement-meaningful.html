1. How can I Make My Prayers More Meaningful?​


Answer:

Singing, repenting your sins, and striving to change your bad habits (sins).

Explanation:

Singing is praying double to God, our Father. During mass, rosary, or novenas, etc, we must sing along with the choir because singing is a form of prayer too, in a more meaningful way.

During our prayers, we must also learn how to own up to our mistakes and repent on them through our prayers. When we pray, it is not only asking for guidance and supplication. We must also adore, praise, thank, and confess (our sins) to God. [ACTS, adoration, confession, thanksgiving, supplication]

Striving to change your bad habits, on the personal note, if you truly are sincere with you prayer, I think it is good when you also do your part on your prayers. 3. How can a person engage with media and information channels in a meaningful manner?Media Information and Literacy po subject​


THE USE OF MEDIA AND INFORMATION CHANNELS

It is very evident that the present world is gifted with so many media and information channels. People nowadays are offered great choices in terms of which media and information channel to use. However, in order to maximize these media and information channels, we must use it in a purposeful way.

First, in order for us to be able to use in media and information channels, we must know first our purpose. By knowing our purpose we are able to direct ourselves to the right media and information channel and use it in a purposeful manner.

Second, observe the netiquette. The netiquette will guide us to act appropriately or to become responsible users of media and information channels. Responsible use of media and information channels engages always in a purposeful manner.

Lastly, understanding the different media and information channels. If we are familiar with the different media and information channels, we can surely use it in a purposeful manner.

10. As a student of NSTP, I can be of better service of my country by?​


You can be of better service of the country by showing and applying what have you learnt in the Program (National Service Training Program). Basically, NSTP aims to teach and train students to develop the civic awareness and have a defense preparedness, if the participant of this program can apply his / her knowledge they gain from the training it is a success for the program itself.  

 Objective of NSTP  

The students are expected to...

Effectively apply their knowledge and skills; and manage themselves in relation to others;  

Promote civic consciousness;  

Develop their physical, moral, spiritual, intellectual and social well-being;  

Inculcate patriotism, nationalism, and advance involvement in public and civic affairs.
