10. good grooming and proper personal hygiene​


Answer:

•brush and floss. Ideally, you should brush your teeth ater every meal

•Wash your hands...

•sleep tight

Answer:

Good personal hygiene habits include:

● washing the body often. If possible, everybody should have a shower or a bath every day. However, there may be times when this is not possible, for example, when people are out camping or there is a shortage of water

● If this happens, a swim or a wash all over the body with a wet sponge or cloth will do

cleaning the teeth at least once a day. Brushing the teeth after each meal is the best way of making sure that gum disease and tooth decay are avoided.

● It is very important to clean teeth after breakfast and immediately before going to bed

● washing the hair with soap or shampoo at least once a week

● washing hands with soap after going to the toilet

● washing hands with soap before preparing and/or eating food. During normal daily activities, such as working and playing, disease causing germs may get onto the hands and under the nails. If the germs are not washed off before preparing food or eating, they may get onto the food

● changing into clean clothes. Dirty clothes should be washed with laundry soap before wearing them again

hanging clothes in the sun to dry. The sun's rays will kill some disease-causing germs and parasites

● turning away from other people and covering the nose and mouth with a tissue or the hand when coughing or sneezing. If this is not done, droplets of liquid containing germs from the nose and mouth will be spread in the air and other people can breathe them in, or the droplets can get onto food

14. Differntiate good grooming from good hygiene​


Answer:

Good hygiene refers to the practices that help maintain cleanliness and prevent the spread of germs and diseases. These practices include washing hands frequently, covering the mouth when coughing or sneezing, using clean towels, and regularly cleaning surfaces and objects that are frequently touched. Good hygiene is essential for maintaining good health and preventing the spread of illnesses.

Good grooming refers to taking care of one's personal hygiene and appearance. This includes regular bathing, washing hair, brushing teeth, trimming nails, and wearing clean and appropriate clothing. Good grooming can help improve self-confidence and make a positive impression on others.

28. Why is it important to have good grooming and hygiene?


Good grooming and hygiene helps you prevent some illnesses. For example, regular handwashing minimizes the risk of catching harmful bacteria or viruses that you could get from the things that you touch. In terms of good grooming, keeping your nails trimmed can also minimize the chances of dirt, bacteria, and other viruses from residing under your long fingernails.

It also helps you feel good about yourself. When you practice good hygiene habits, like taking a shower regularly, you will feel fresh and clean most of the time. People who also take time to have their nails or hair done feel like it is a reward for them because they worked hard. It helps them relieve stress, enhancing their mental wellbeing, although they may not realize it.

It is also beneficial in maintaining the general health and wellbeing of the public. When people follow good hygiene and grooming habits, they raise the health standards of the society. Take the outbreak of cholera for example. It started with people not paying attention to the cleanliness of their surroundings, so much so that latrines were just a few feet away from their sources of water. This contaminated the water that they use. They didn't wash their hands regularly as well, which contributed to the spread of the disease. If many people do not follow such safety and cleanliness standards, everyone suffers. Conversely, if everyone does his or her part in maintaining personal cleanliness, they can stop the spread of diseases together.
