22. 1: what are the commonly asked interview question?2: what are the things are judged in an interview?3: what are the common mistakes people make in an interview?​


Answer:What are the commonly asked interview questions?

Tell me about yourself.

What are your strengths and weaknesses?

Why do you want to work for our company?

What experience do you have in this field?

What are your long-term career goals?

Can you give an example of a time when you had to overcome a challenge?

How do you handle stress and pressure?

What motivates you in your work?

How do you work in a team?

Do you have any questions for us?

What are the things judged in an interview?

Communication skills: how well the candidate can articulate their thoughts and ideas.

Qualifications and experience: whether the candidate has the required skills and experience for the job.

Attitude and personality: whether the candidate has a positive attitude and personality that would fit with the company culture.

Problem-solving ability: how well the candidate can think on their feet and solve problems.

Interpersonal skills: how well the candidate can work with others and build relationships.

Adaptability: whether the candidate can adapt to new situations and learn quickly.

Leadership potential: whether the candidate has the potential to lead others and take on leadership roles in the future.

What are the common mistakes people make in an interview?

Arriving late or unprepared.

Not researching the company or the job before the interview.

Being too nervous or not confident enough.

Talking too much or not enough.

Not giving specific examples or details when answering questions.

Speaking negatively about previous employers or coworkers.

Failing to ask questions or show interest in the company or job.

Not dressing appropriately for the interview.

Focusing too much on salary or benefits rather than the job itself.

Forgetting to follow up with a thank-you note or email after the interview.
