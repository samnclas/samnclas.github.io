3. EM waves application or uses of radio waves


THE RADIO WAVES

In electromagnetic spectrum, radio wave has the longest wavelength, but it is also the wave with the lowest frequency. In visible light spectrum radio waves represent the color red within the rainbow-like range of colors.

APPLICATIONS

Radio waves are used for communications, satellites, telecommunication towers and cell sites, this wave travels in the medium (the air), and then when it reaches the tower or satellite, it will then convert the wave into vibrations, producing sound waves or signals that can be turned into picture or video via computer.
