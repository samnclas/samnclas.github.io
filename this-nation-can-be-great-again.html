1. Ipaliwanag ang "This nation can be great again"​


Explanation:

Ang pangungusap na "This nation can be great again" ay nangangahulugang ang bansang ito ay maaaring maging mahusay muli o maging magaling ulit sa iba't ibang larangan tulad ng ekonomiya, edukasyon, kalusugan, at iba pa. Maaaring ginagamit ito upang magbigay ng inspirasyon sa mga mamamayan na labanan ang mga hamon at suliraning kinakaharap ng kanilang bansa, tulad ng kahirapan, kawalan ng trabaho, at korapsyon.

Sa pangkalahatan, ang pangungusap na ito ay nagpapahiwatig ng isang pangako o layunin na magtulungan ang mga tao upang maabot ang isang mas magandang kinabukasan para sa kanilang bansa. Ito ay maaaring maging isang hamon para sa mga lider ng bansa upang magkaroon ng maayos na pamamahala at magpakita ng mga hakbang upang mapalakas ang mga sektor ng bansa, o maging inspirasyon para sa mga tao na magkaisa at magtrabaho nang sama-sama upang mapaunlad ang kanilang bansa.

Answer:

"You're What You Eat" - This slogan promotes the idea that the food we consume has a direct impact on our health and wellbeing. It emphasizes the importance of choosing healthy foods to maintain a healthy body and mind.

"Water is life, don't waste it" - This slogan emphasizes the importance of water as a vital resource for life and encourages people to use it wisely and conserve it.

"Our earth is the only thing we've got, save it" - This slogan reminds us of the importance of preserving the planet and protecting it from harm. It encourages people to take actions to reduce their impact on the environment and help save our planet.

"This nation can be great again" - This slogan conveys a message of hope and optimism for the future of a country. It suggests that by working together, the people of the nation can make it great once more.

"Save a tree for life" - This slogan promotes the idea of conserving trees, which are vital for the health of our planet. It encourages people to take actions to protect trees, such as reducing paper usage and planting new trees.

a. Diosdado Macapagal

b. Manuel Roxas

c. Ferdinand Marcos

d. Ramon Magsaysay

Answer:

C. Ferdinand Marcos

"This nation can be great again. This i have said over and over. It is my articles of faith, and divine providence has willed that you and i can now translate this faith into deeds."
