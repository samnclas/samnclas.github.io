Use blue light blocking glasses: These glasses are designed to filter out blue light and can be worn while using electronic devices, particularly in the evenings when the body's natural circadian rhythm is preparing for sleep.

Adjust the color temperature of electronic devices: Many modern devices have a "night mode" or "blue light filter" setting that adjusts the color temperature to reduce the amount of blue light emitted.

This may include turning off devices an hour or two before bed, using low-wattage bulbs in the bedroom, and avoiding activities that are mentally stimulating before bedtime.

27. His eyes were hollows of madness, his hair like moldy hay. What two poetic techniques are used in this sentence?


Answer:

The two poetic techniques used in this sentence are personification and imagery.

Personification is a literary device in which human qualities or characteristics are attributed to non-human things, such as animals, objects, or abstract concepts. In this sentence, the eyes are described as "hollows of madness," which gives them a human quality and implies that they are expressive and capable of conveying emotion.

Imagery is a technique that involves using descriptive language to create vivid mental pictures or sensory experiences in the reader's mind. In this sentence, the hair is described as being "like moldy hay," which creates a vivid image of something that is old, dry, and unhealthy.
