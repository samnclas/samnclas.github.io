let x be the number

twice the square of the sum of a number and 3 is 98, in figures,

2(x+3)²=98

to find x, first, divide both sides by 2 to eliminate that 2.

2(x+3)²/2=98/2

square root both sides

√(x+3)²=√49

transpose 4 to the right side

x+4-4=7-4

and we'll get
