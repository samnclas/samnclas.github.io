19. general to specific vs. specific to general sentence about school​


Answer:

General to specific sentence: Education is important for personal and social development. In school, students learn a variety of subjects such as mathematics, science, and history.

Specific to general sentence: Our school has a great mathematics program. Students who excel in mathematics are likely to succeed in many fields, including science, engineering, and finance.
