Detail Explanation

Lice are small, wingless parasites that can cause dermatitis (red, itchy and swollen). There are several symptoms of lice, namely: Experiencing itching on the head, There are small red bumps on the scalp, neck and shoulders, There are nits on the hair roots and cannot be swept away with a comb.

There are several intermediaries for the spread of lice, namely direct contact between lice sufferers in the form of head with head or body with body and using items simultaneously for example in the form of combs, hats, hoods and so on.

The presence of lice is very disturbing and makes humans uncomfortable in carrying out their activities, therefore we can avoid it by adopting a healthy life.
