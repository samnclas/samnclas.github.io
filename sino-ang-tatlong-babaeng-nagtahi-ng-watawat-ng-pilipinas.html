5. SINO ANG TATLONG BABAENG NAGTAHI NG WATAWAT


Sino ang nagtahi ng watawat ng Pilipinas?

           Ang mga unang tao na nagtahi ng watawat ng Pilipinas ay sina Marcella Marina Agoncillo, ang kanyang batang anak na noon ay limang taong gulang lamang na si Lorenza, at ang pamangkin ni Dr. Jose Rizal na si Delfina Herbosa de Natividad. Ayon sa kasaysayan, ang watawat ng Pilipinas ay ginawa nila sa bansang HongKong taong 1898.

Marcella Marina Agoncillo

               Marcela Coronel Mario married Felipe Encarnacion Agoncillo, a Filipino lawyer and jurist, when she was 30 years old, and they had six children. Her marriage had a significant impact on Philippine history. When her husband was exiled in Hong Kong during the Philippine Revolution, Marcela Mario Agoncillo and the rest of the family followed him and briefly remained there to avoid the occupying Spain's anti-Filipino hostilities. General Emilio Aguinaldo asked her to embroider the flag that would signify the Republic of the Philippines while she was in Hong Kong.

             

Lorenza Agoncillo

              She was one of three ladies responsible for designing the first Philippine flag. According to legend, in March or April 1898, Aguinaldo asked Marcela  to create the Philippine flag based on a design influenced by the Cuban flag provided by the revolutionary committee. Lorenza, who was seven years old at the time, assisted her mother in making the flag alongside Rizal's niece, The project was completed in five days. When Aguinaldo traveled back to the Philippines on the US ship McCulloch after defeating the Spanish fleet in Manila Bay, he brought the flag with him. It was waved from Aguinaldo's residence in Kawit, Cavite, after he declared Philippine independence on June 12, 1898.

             

Delfina Herbosa de Natividad

Was a Filipino renowned for being one of the three women, together with Marcela Agoncillo and her daughter Lorenza, who seamed together the Philippine flag, and for being the niece of the National Hero of the Philippines, José Rizal.
