Type of Sounds

There are three types of sounds, infrasonic, audiosonic, and ultrasonic.

1. Infrasonic

Infrasonic is a type of sound that cannot be heard by humans. The frequency of infrasonic is below 20 Hz. Animals that can hear infrasonic are dolphins, elephants, and dogs. Examples of infrasonic sounds are the activity of volcanoes and earthquakes which will be recorded by seismographs.

2. Audiosonic

Audiosonic is a type of sound that can be caught and heard by the human ear. The frequency of this sound ranges from 20Hz-20,000Hz. Examples of audiosonic are the sound of musical instruments, the sound of people whispering, the sound of people talking, and so on.

3. Ultrasonic

Ultrasonic is a type of sound that is very loud, but cannot be heard by the human ear. The frequency of ultrasonic sound is above 20,000Hz. This sound is used to perform ultrasound or ultrasound. Animals that can hear this type of sound are whales, bats, mice, and sloth.

Facts about Sound :

Generally, there are two types of sound, audible sound and inaudible sound. Audible sound is a sound that can heard and caught by human ears. Human can only hear the sounds with frequency 20Hz - 20.000Hz. Then, inaudible sound is a sound that can not be heard by human ears, it might because it is too soft or too loud to hear. The frequencies of inaudible sounds are below 20Hz and above 20.000Hz.
