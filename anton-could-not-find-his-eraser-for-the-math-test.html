28. Anton is 4yrs older than Liza. The product of his age 3yrs now and Liza age 2yrs ago is 90 years . Find their present ages


Present ages:

Liza: x   ⇒   Liza's age 2 years ago: x-2

Anton: x+4  ⇒   Anton's age in 3 years: x+4+3  or  x+7

Equation:

(x+7) (x-2) = 90

x² -2x + 7x - 14 - 90 = 0

x² + 5x - 104 = 0

Factor the equation:

(x - 8) (x + 13) = 0

x - 8 = 0

x = 8

x - 13 = 0

x = -13

Choose the positive solution, x = 8.

Substitute:

Liza: x = 8 years old

Anton: 8+4 = 12 years old

ANSWER: Liza is 8 years old and Anto is 12 years old.

Check:

The product of Anto's age in 3 years and Liza's age 2 years ago is 90.

(12+3) (8-2) = 90

(15)(6) = 90

90 = 90   (True)
