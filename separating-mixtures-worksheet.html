WORKBOOK

Aside from text, and worksheet, workbook is one of the most fundamental components of Microsoft Excel that we must learn.

Like every other application, a workbook is a separate file. One or more worksheets are included in each workbook. A workbook can also be a single worksheet or a collection of many worksheets.

You can adjust the arrangement of your worksheets within the workbook, add or delete worksheets, hide them without deleting them, and hide them without deleting them.
