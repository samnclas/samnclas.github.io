Conjecture:

The product of two even numbers will always be even.

Example:

2 x 4 = 8 (product is even)

Conjecture:

The product of two even numbers will always be divisible by 4.

Example:

6 x 8 = 48 (product is divisible by 4)

Conjecture:

The quotient of two integers with a different sign will always be negative.
