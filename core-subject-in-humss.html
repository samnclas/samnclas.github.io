3. subject: world religion11 humss ( I need this tommorow) Discuss briefly the important concepts of Judaism: 1. CORE TEACHINGS 2. SACRED SCRIPTURES 3. PRACTICES 4. BELIEFS AND DOCTRINES5. RELATED ISSUES​


Judaism is an Abrahamic religion that originated in the Middle East over 3,500 years ago. Some of the important concepts of Judaism are:

CORE TEACHINGS: The core teachings of Judaism revolve around monotheism, the belief in one God. The Jewish faith teaches that God is the creator and sustainer of the universe, and that humans are created in God's image. Additionally, Judaism emphasizes the importance of ethical behavior, social justice, and the pursuit of knowledge.

SACRED SCRIPTURES: The Jewish scriptures are composed of the Hebrew Bible, which includes the Torah, the Prophets, and the Writings. The Torah is considered the most sacred text in Judaism and contains the first five books of the Hebrew Bible. It is believed to have been revealed to Moses by God at Mount Sinai.

PRACTICES: Some of the central practices of Judaism include prayer, studying Torah, observing the Sabbath, and following the dietary laws of kashrut. The Sabbath, which begins on Friday evening and ends on Saturday evening, is a day of rest and worship. The dietary laws of kashrut include the prohibition of eating certain foods, such as pork and shellfish, and the requirement to slaughter animals in a specific way.

BELIEFS AND DOCTRINES: Some of the key beliefs and doctrines of Judaism include the belief in the coming of the Messiah, the resurrection of the dead, and the idea of a final judgment. Additionally, Jews believe in the concept of tikkun olam, or the repair of the world, and the idea that humans have a responsibility to work towards making the world a better place.

RELATED ISSUES: Some of the related issues in Judaism include the relationship between Judaism and other religions, the role of women in Jewish society, and the ongoing debate over the status of the State of Israel as a Jewish state. Additionally, there are various sects within Judaism, each with their own beliefs and practices, such as Orthodox, Conservative, and Reform Judaism.

13. what is the core subject of senior highschool?​


Answer:

The core subjects of senior high school depend on the specific track or strand that the student chooses. In the Philippines, the Department of Education implements the K-12 program, which offers four tracks in senior high school:

1. Academic Track (which includes three strands):

- Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM)

- Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM)

- Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

2. Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track (which includes several strands)

3. Sports Track

4. Arts and Design Track

Each track has its own set of core subjects that are specific to the track. In general, the core subjects in senior high school include English, Filipino, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Physical Education. However, the specific subjects and their contents may vary depending on the track or strand.
