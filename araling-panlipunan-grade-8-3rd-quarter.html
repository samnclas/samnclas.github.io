4. Reflection 3rd quarter Science grade 8 essay patulonh plsss​


Answer:

The 3rd quarter of Science for Grade 8 students focuses on the topic of Earth and Space Science. In this quarter, we learned about the different components of the Earth, including its layers, plate tectonics, and natural disasters such as earthquakes and volcanic eruptions. We also studied the solar system and the different celestial bodies that make it up, including the planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.

One of the most fascinating things I learned during this quarter was the concept of plate tectonics. I found it interesting to learn how the Earth's crust is made up of several large tectonic plates that move and interact with each other, causing earthquakes and volcanic activity. I also learned about the different types of plate boundaries, including divergent, convergent, and transform boundaries.

Another topic that caught my attention was the study of the solar system. I was amazed to learn about the different planets and their characteristics, such as their size, composition, and distance from the sun. I also found it intriguing to learn about the different types of celestial bodies, such as asteroids and comets, and their potential impact on Earth.

Throughout this quarter, I also developed my skills in data analysis, critical thinking, and problem-solving. I learned how to interpret graphs and diagrams and use them to draw conclusions about the Earth and the solar system. I also developed my ability to ask questions, make observations, and test hypotheses, which is critical in the field of science.

Overall, the 3rd quarter of Science for Grade 8 was an enriching experience. I gained a deeper understanding of the Earth and the solar system and developed various skills that will be useful in my future studies. I look forward to learning more about science and the world around us in the next quarter.

6. Ano yung mga topics sa grade 8 filipino 3rd quarter ​


Ang mga posibleng mga topic sa Grade 8 Filipino para sa ikatlong quarter ay maaaring mag-focus sa mga sumusunod:

1. Panitikan: Pag-aaral ng iba't ibang anyo ng panitikan tulad ng nobela, maikling kwento, tula, sanaysay, at iba pa. Maaaring pag-aralan ang mga halimbawa ng panitikan mula sa mga kilalang manunulat ng bansa.

2. Pag-unawa sa Teksto: Pagsusuri sa mga tekstong nababasa upang maunawaan kung ano ang ibig sabihin nito. Maaaring mag-focus ito sa mga pormal na tekstong pang-akademiko tulad ng mga aklat, artikulo, at iba pa.

3. Pagsasalita: Pagpapakita ng kakayahang magpakipag-usap sa mga kapwa estudyante at guro sa wikang Filipino. Maaaring mag-focus ito sa pagpapakita ng mga kaugalian sa pakikipagtalastasan at paggamit ng tamang tono at intonasyon sa pagsasalita.

4. Pagsulat: Pagpapakita ng kakayahang magsulat ng mga komposisyon sa wikang Filipino tulad ng sanaysay, talumpati, at iba pa. Maaaring mag-focus ito sa mga kaugalian sa pagsulat tulad ng pagpili ng tamang salita at estruktura ng pangungusap.

5. Wika: Pagpapakita ng kakayahang magamit ng wasto at epektibo ang wikang Filipino sa iba't ibang sitwasyon. Maaaring mag-focus ito sa mga kaugalian at kasanayan sa paggamit ng wikang Filipino sa lipunan at sa mga pang-araw-araw na sitwasyon.

Answer:

Ang mga topics sa Grade 8 Filipino para sa 3rd quarter ay maaaring mag-iba depende sa kurikulum ng bawat paaralan at lugar. Gayunpaman, narito ang ilang mga pangkalahatang mga paksa na itinuturo sa Grade 8 Filipino 3rd quarter:

Pagbabalangkas ng Sulatin - Pag-aaral sa paggawa ng balangkas sa pagsulat ng iba't ibang uri ng sulating akademiko.Talumpati - Pag-aaral sa mga estratehiya sa pagsulat at pagbigkas ng isang talumpati.Tula - Pag-aaral sa mga salita at teknik na ginagamit sa pagtula, mga anyo ng tula, at ang pagsulat ng sariling tula.Sanaysay - Pag-aaral sa iba't ibang uri ng sanaysay tulad ng sanaysay pormal, sanaysay ng opinyon, at sanaysay na deskriptiv.Nobela - Pag-aaral sa mga elemento ng nobela tulad ng tauhan, tagpuan, suliranin, at paglalahad ng kwento.Pagsasalin - Pag-aaral sa mga estratehiya sa pagsasalin ng mga teksto mula sa Filipino papunta sa iba't ibang wika at mula sa iba't ibang wika papunta sa Filipino.

28. my reflection in 3rd quarter math grade 8 essay​


The third quarter in mathematics for us grade 8 students is a crucial period as it builds on the foundational concepts learned in the first and second quarters. The focus of the third quarter in mathematics is typically on algebraic equations, functions, and geometry.

One of the main topics covered in the third quarter of eighth grade mathematics is algebraic equations. We learned how to write and solve equations involving one or more variables. We are also introduced to the concept of linear equations and how to graph them on a coordinate plane. This topic is essential as it forms the basis for higher-level mathematics and science courses.

Another critical area of focus in the third quarter is functions. We learned how to analyze and graph different types of functions, including linear, quadratic, and exponential functions. We also learned how to solve problems involving functions and apply them to real-life situations. This concept is essential in understanding the relationship between variables and how they change over time.

Geometry is another significant topic covered in the third quarter of eighth grade mathematics. We learned about the properties of lines, angles, and polygons. We also studied the Pythagorean theorem, which is crucial in understanding the relationship between the sides of a right triangle. The concepts of similarity and congruence are also introduced, which are essential in later courses such as trigonometry.

In addition to these topics, the third quarter of eighth grade mathematics also emphasizes problem-solving skills. We are given various problems that require us to apply the concepts that we have learned to real-world situations. Also, we are encouraged to think critically, logically, and creatively to find solutions to these problems.

To summarize, the third quarter of eighth grade mathematics is a crucial period in which, as a student, you learn about algebraic equations, functions, and geometry. These concepts are essential in understanding the relationship between variables, solving problems, and applying mathematical principles to real-world situations. The problem-solving skills developed during this quarter will serve as the foundation for success in higher-level mathematics courses.
