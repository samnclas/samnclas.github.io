24. A blue neon light blinks every 4 seconds. a red one blinks every 5 seconds while a green one blinks every 6 seconds. how many times will they blink together in half an hour?


Let: x = the number of times they'll blink

Solution:

(4/60)x + (5/60)x + (6/60)x = 30

(1/4)x = 30

x = 30 ÷ 1/4

x = 120

The light blinks 120 times in 30minutes or half an hour

Note: 1 minute = 60 seconds

30minutes = 1/2 hour

28. two blinking lights are turned in at the same time. One blinks every 5 seconds and the other blinks every 6 seconds. in 60 seconds, how many times will they blink at the same time?


2 because there's only 2 numbers in 1-60 that can be divided by 5

Since we're counting the times that they're blinking at the same time, we're going to ignore the fact that the first one blinks 12 times and the second one 10 times. Instead, we're going to get how they're going to blink at the same time.

So firstly, 60 seconds can be divided by 5 and 6. Since that's the case, when are they going to blink? To start, when can the two numbers be divided at the same time in 60? That's at 30 and 60. So when the timer hits 30 and 60 seconds, both of the lights blink.

So, both of the lights blink at the same time, precisely 2 times within 60 seconds.
