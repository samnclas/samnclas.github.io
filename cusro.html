11. A solution contains 2.7.g of CUSO, in 75mL of solution. What is the percent (mass/volume) of the solution?


SOLUTION:

Step 1: List the given values.

[tex]\begin{aligned} & mass_{\text{solute}} = \text{2.7 g} \\ & V_{\text{solution}} = \text{75 mL} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Step 2: Calculate the percent by mass per volume.

[tex]\begin{aligned} \%(m/v) & = \frac{mass_{\text{solute}}}{V_{\text{solution}}} \times 100\% \\ & = \frac{\text{2.7 g}}{\text{75 mL}} \times 100\% \\ & = \boxed{3.6\%} \end{aligned}[/tex]

Hence, the percent by mass per volume concentration of the solution is 3.6%.

30. What is the percentage composition by mass of sulfur in a CuSO⁴​


Step 1: Calculate the molar mass of sodium sulfate.

molar mass = (22.99 g/mol × 2) + (32.07 g/mol × 1) + (16.00 g/mol × 4)

molar mass = 142.05 g/mol

Step 2: Calculate the percentage composition of sulfur in sodium sulfate.

For %S

\text{\%S} = \frac{\text{32.07 g/mol × 1}}{\text{142.05 g/mol}} × 100%S=

142.05 g/mol

32.07 g/mol × 1

×100

\boxed{\text{\%S = 22.58\%}}

%S = 22.58%
