8. write one page reaction paper given the title "essence of choosing an appropriate quantitative research design"​


Essence of choosing an appropriate quantitative research design

Since quantitative research is more scientific, objective, rapid, focused, and accepted, it is chosen over qualitative research. It's utilized to define the problem or to come up with a solution. The sort of study design you select is critical because it serves as a foundation for making subsequent decisions about your research plan, from the setting and participants through the analysis and presentation of the results. Quantitative research is defined by the use of a 'theory' to frame and so comprehend the topic at hand. Its starting point, if not its main aim, might be to contribute to theory development. It is based on the premise that knowledge of reality may be achieved "through the eyes of the researcher."

In most circumstances, a good quantitative research design includes a tailored combination of data collection techniques, such as online surveys (web, mobile, and email), direct (postal) mail surveys, point-of-purchase surveys, and, in some cases, telephone surveys.
