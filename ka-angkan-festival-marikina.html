26. Marikina celebrates Sapatos Festival. In a certain booth, there are 10 new shoe designs to be displayed. In how many ways can these shoes be arranged if there are only 7 spaces available in a row?


Answer:

The number of ways to arrange 10 shoes in 7 spaces is equal to the number of combinations of 10 items taken 7 at a time, which can be calculated using the formula:

C(10,7) = 10! / (7! * 3!) = 120.

Therefore, there are 120 different ways to arrange the 10 shoes in 7 spaces.

A permutation is an arrangement of all or some of the objects in a set. Where, orders and matters.

Formula for PERMUTATION

P(n, r) = n!/(n - r)!

The number of permutation of n objects taken r at a time.

Where:
