[tex] {4y}^{2} - 256 = 0[/tex]

Transpose 256 and change its sign

[tex] {4y}^{2} = 256[/tex]

Divide both sides by 4

[tex] \frac{4 {y}^{2} }{4} = \frac{256}{4} [/tex]

[tex] {y}^{2} = 64[/tex]

Get the square root of 64

[tex]y = \sqrt{64} [/tex]

[tex]y = 8 \: or \: y = - 8[/tex]
