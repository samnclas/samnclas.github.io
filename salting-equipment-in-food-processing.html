21. Directions: Based on the table below, identify the tools and equipment needed inFood/Fish Processing. Explain each in terms of their use.Use/PreparationToolsMethod ofProcessingMeasuringDevicesCuttingImplementsCookingEquipment1. Salting2. Canning3. Drying4. Freezing5. Curing​


Answer:

1.Salting- -Salting is the Preservation of Food with dry edible salt. It is related to pickling in general and more specifically to brining and is one form of curing. It is one of the oldest methods of preserving food, and two historically significant salt-cured foods are salted fish and salt-cured meat.2.Canning- -Canning is a Method of preserving Food in which the food contents are processed and sealed in an airtight container. Canning provides a shelf life that typically ranges from one to five years, although under specific circumstances, it can be much longer.3. Drying - -is a mass transfer process consisting of the removal of water or another solvent by evaporation from a solid, semi-solid or liquid. This process is often used as a final production step before selling or packaging products. 4. Freezing-- is a phase transition where a liquid turns into a solid when its temperature is lowered below its freezing point. In accordance with the internationally established definition, freezing means the solidification phase change of a liquid or the liquid content of a substance, usually due to cooling. 5. Curing-- is any of various Food Preservasion and flavoring processes of foods such as meat, fish and vegetables, by the addition of salt, with the aim of drawing moisture out of the food by the process of osmosis.
