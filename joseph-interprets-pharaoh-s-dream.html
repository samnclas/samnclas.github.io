5. give 3 dreams joseph had dreamed about Joseph King of Dreams​


Answer:

Joseph, the King of Dreams, had three significant dreams. In the first dream, he saw himself and his brothers binding sheaves of wheat, and his brothers' sheaves bowed down to his. In the second dream, he saw the sun, moon, and eleven stars bowing down to him. In the third dream, he saw himself and his brothers as stars, with his star shining the brightest. These dreams would later prove to be prophetic and play a significant role in Joseph's life.
