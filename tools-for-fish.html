4. A tool cutting meat poultry and fish


Back to Industry

Become a Meat, Poultry, or Fish Cutter or Trimmer

Meat, Poultry, and Fish Cutters and Trimmers are workers who cut and trim poultry, fish and different varieties of meat. In doing so, they make use of a wide range of tools such as cleavers, meat saws and knives. In this role, they carefully cut and trim meat as per the specifications. they separate the undesired components such as excess fats or damaged portions of the meat. A part of their job is to cut and trim the meat by observing standard meat cuts. they then categorize meat into different parts, weight the parts and place them in sealed containers or wrap them in packages.

How do I become a Meat, Poultry, or Fish Cutter or Trimmer

The basic requirement for a career as Meat, Poultry, and Fish Cutters and Trimmers are skills and qualifications in Production, Processing, Food Production, Public Safety and Security, Administration, Management, or a related field.

Sample Roles

Beef trimmer

A Beef Trimmer is someone who makes use of a wide range of hand tools to cut and trim beef. Such a worker may cut beef into standard parts and portions which can be sold at retail locations.

Fish processor

A Fish Processor is someone who performs various tasks related to processing and preparing a fish before it can be packaged. Such tasks include scaling the fish, cleaning it and filleting it as per the requirements.
