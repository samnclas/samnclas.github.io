[tex]12 \div 300 = 0.04 \times 100 = 4\%[/tex]
[tex]4\% < 8\%[/tex]
therefore, the machine is qualified.The grinding machine was qualified for a particular task.

[tex]\frac{12}{300}(100)=0.04(100)=4[/tex]

The defective parts produced were 4% which was less than the 8% disqualification limit. Data showed that the machine was qualified.

22. 2. A man is engaged in buy and sell. He sold agrinding machine for 88,500. If his profit is15% of the purchased cost, find thepurchased cost of the machine.​


Answer:

The purchased cost of the machine is 76,956.

Step-by-step explanation:

Purchased cost of Machine: x

Profit: 15%(x) or 0.15x

Selling price:  88,500

Find the purchased cost, x:

x + 0.15x = 88,500

1.15x = 88,500

1.15x/1.15 = 88,500/1.15

x ≈ 76,956

The purchased cost of the machine is 76,956.
