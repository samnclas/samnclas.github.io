As a teacher, I think the learning modality do students prefer the most is HYBRID LEARNING (learning methods that combine or combine online learning with face-to-face learning).

EXPLANATION:

Types of Learning Modalities are several types of student participation methods in the teaching process, consisting of 3, namely:

In-personRemoteHybrids

Several schools around the world operating under the covid-19 pandemic have adapted quickly to gather information about how each student participates in teaching for each school day.

Learning strategies since the pandemic period that are often referred to are Hybrid Learning and blended learning. The two terms are often referred to the same thing, even though hybrid learning and blended learning have differences in their implementation. What is hybrid learning? Hybrid learning is a learning model in which a teacher teaches students who are learning from home and also learning at school simultaneously with the help of technology. In other words, Hybrid learning is a model or learning method that combines face-to-face learning systems with online-based learning. And the proportion used is usually balanced, which is 50% of both.

How about blended learning? The learning strategy of blended learning is a learning process that combines direct teaching and learning activities (synchronous) with a synchronous or asynchronous. Learning using blended learning can be done remotely.

The advantages of hybrid learning are as follows:

Students can choose whether to take online or offline learning. Especially at this time offline learning must be with the permission of the students' parents.Students can feel the variation in learning.Students who choose to study online or students who are constrained cannot attend class can still attend the presentation of material or learning activities that are guided directly by the teacher at school.There are restrictions on physical activities and social interactions which are highly recommended to prevent the spread of the virus.
