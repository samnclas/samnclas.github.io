13. ANSWER THE FOLLOWING QUESTION IN AN ESSAY FORM1, What is the importance of dessert?2. What is your favorite dessert? and why


Answer:

What is the importance of dessert?

Dessert is an important part of a meal that adds a sweet and satisfying ending to the dining experience. It serves as a reward for a delicious meal and can bring people together to share in the enjoyment of something sweet. Desserts come in many forms such as cakes, pastries, ice cream, and fruit, and each one has its unique characteristics and flavors.

Apart from being delicious, desserts also provide various health benefits. Fruits like berries, apples, and grapes are often used in desserts and are rich in vitamins, antioxidants, and fiber. These nutrients help boost the immune system, promote healthy digestion, and reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as cancer, diabetes, and heart disease. Additionally, some desserts like dark chocolate have been shown to improve cognitive function, lower blood pressure, and reduce stress levels.

Desserts also play an essential role in celebrations and cultural events. Many festivals and holidays around the world are centered around the preparation and sharing of sweets, like Christmas cookies or Diwali sweets. These desserts help to create a sense of tradition, togetherness, and happiness in families and communities.

Overall, the importance of dessert goes beyond just satisfying a sweet tooth. They bring people together, provide essential nutrients, and are an essential part of celebrations and cultural events.

What is your favorite dessert? and why?

My favorite dessert is chocolate cake. The rich, moist cake and the creamy, chocolatey frosting create a perfect combination of flavors that is impossible to resist. Every bite feels like a heavenly indulgence, and I can't help but feel a sense of comfort and happiness when I eat it.

I love that chocolate cake can be enjoyed in various ways, whether it's a small slice after dinner or a large piece shared with friends and family during a special occasion. The versatility of chocolate cake is also appealing to me, as it can be customized with different toppings, flavors, and decorations to suit my taste.

Additionally, chocolate has been shown to have several health benefits, including reducing inflammation, improving heart health, and boosting brain function. While I know that chocolate cake is not the healthiest dessert option, I can enjoy it in moderation and still reap some of the health benefits of chocolate.

Overall, my love for chocolate cake goes beyond just the taste. It brings me joy, comfort, and a sense of indulgence, and I always look forward to enjoying a slice whenever I can.

28. Create a recipe book of different desserts and dessert sauces. Include the following:* Picture of dessert/dessert sauces* Materials/ ingredients* Equipment/tools* Procedureall people needs this now so answer this corretly


Answer:

APPLE PIE RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

2 cups all purpose flour

2 tbsp white sugar

1 tsp fine salt

1 bar butter, cut into small cubes

½ cup cold water

½ tsp vinegar

2 tbsp all purpose flour for dusting

1 tsp butter for greasing

¼ cup butter

½ cup light brown sugar

½ cup Nestlé All Purpose Cream

¼ tsp fine salt

6 pcs apple, peeled and sliced

½ tbsp cinnamon powder

2 tbsp cornstarch

1 pc small egg, beaten for egg wash

½ pack Nestlé All Purpose Cream, chilled

1 tbsp sugar

PROCEDURE

Step 1: Pre-heat oven to 350ᴼF. (1 min)

Step 2: For the crust, combine flour, sugar, salt and butter. Mix with finger tips until small pea-

size butter is achieved. Mix water and vinegar. Slowly mix in with the flour mixture using finger

tips. Knead until just combined. Divide into 2 portions, form into a ball and refrigerate for 10

minutes. (20 mins)

Step 3: Melt butter in a pan. Stir in brown sugar, Nestlé All Purpose Cream and salt. Gently heat

for 2 minutes while mixing. Transfer into a large bowl to slightly cool. (10 mins)

Step 4: Add apple slices, cinnamon powder and cornstarch. Mix well and set aside. (5 mins)

Step 5: Dust the one of the dough with flour and roll into a round 2-inch bigger than the pie pan.

Grease a 7-inch pie with butter. Place the rolled out dough and lightly press evenly into the

bottom and sides of the pie pan. Roll out the other dough and cut into ¾-inch strips. (10 mins)

Step 6: Spread the apple mixture on the pie crust. Cover with strips of dough making a lattice

pattern. Seal the sides with a fork. Brush with eggwasg and bake in the oven for 45 minutes. Set

aside for 10 minutes. (1 hr)

Step 7: Whip chilled Nestlé All Purpose Cream with 1 tbsp sugar until stiff peaks. Serve with

Creamy Apple Pie. (5 mins)
