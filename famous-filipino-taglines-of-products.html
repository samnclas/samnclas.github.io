Product: Cream Silk hair conditioner

Tagline: "Beyond beautiful hair"

The information in the tagline that is biased is the phrase "beyond beautiful hair". This is biased because it implies that the use of the product will result in hair that is not just beautiful but also goes beyond what is expected or typical. This creates an unrealistic expectation of the product's effectiveness and may mislead consumers into thinking that the product will automatically make their hair look and feel a certain way, regardless of their hair type or condition.
