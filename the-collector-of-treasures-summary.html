15. The sadness collector story meaning?


The short story, The Sorrow Collector, is about a girl named Rica who has trouble growing up with her father because her mother works as a domestic helper outside of the country. When her mother went to Paris, she used her affection and her presence to give various gifts to Rica.

This story tells about Rica's problem while growing up with her father just because her mother honors an overseas contract as a domestic helper. To Rica it still tastes salty, like tears, and the strange look of her father every time she asks him to read the letters from Paris to her again.


16. what is a work collector​


Answer:

A work collector recovers overdue payments on accounts. Collectors track debtors down by phone or mail and assist them to make payments by negotiating repayment plans, and encouraging them to find alternative payment solutions.
